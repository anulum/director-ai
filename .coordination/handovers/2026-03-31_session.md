## 3. Tokenizer Fix — "Already borrowed" RuntimeError

### Status: COMPLETE

Root cause: tokenizers ≥0.21 fast tokenizer has reentrancy bug with DeBERTa
SentencePiece models. The `_tokenizer.no_padding()` call fails when the
tokenizer is "borrowed" by another thread/context.

Fix (2 files):
- `src/director_ai/core/scoring/nli.py:150` — `AutoTokenizer.from_pretrained(model_name, use_fast=False)`
- `src/director_ai/core/scoring/scorer.py:304` — same fix for judge tokenizer

Impact: E2E benchmark on RX 6600 XT now runs without RuntimeError.
