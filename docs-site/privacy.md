## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 PyPI Package Downloads

We do **not** collect telemetry, usage data, or analytics from our PyPI packages. `pip install director-ai` does not phone home.

### 3.2 Documentation Sites (GitHub Pages)

Our documentation sites (e.g., `anulum.github.io/director-ai/`) are static sites hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, referrer). See [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or cookies on our documentation sites.

### 3.3 Email Communications

When you email us or we email you (e.g., partnership inquiries, outreach), we store:

- Your email address and name
- Email content and attachments
- Timestamps

We use this data solely to respond to your inquiry and manage business relationships.

### 3.4 GitHub Interactions

When you open issues, pull requests, or discussions on our GitHub repositories, GitHub processes that data under [GitHub's terms](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

### 3.5 Commercial Licenses

If you purchase a commercial license (via [Polar](https://polar.sh/anulum)), Polar processes payment data under [Polar's Privacy Policy](https://polar.sh/legal/privacy). We receive your name, email, and license tier. We do not store payment card details.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share data with:

- **GitHub** — repository hosting, issue tracking, CI/CD

- **Email correspondence:** retained for the duration of the business relationship, then deleted within 12 months of last contact.
- **License records:** retained for the duration of the license plus any legally required retention period.
- **GitHub data:** retained as long as the repository exists, governed by GitHub's policies.
